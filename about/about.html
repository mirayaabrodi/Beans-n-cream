Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee
served in small, strong shots and is the base for
many coffee drinks.

Macchiato

Caffè macchiato, sometimes called espresso
macchiato, is an espresso coffee drink with a small
amount of milk, usually foamed.

ristretto

Ristretto is a "short shot" (30 ml from a double
basket) of a more highly concentrated
espresso coffee.

capuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee
drink that originated in Italy, and is traditionally
prepared with steamed milk foam (microfoam).

Frappe

A frappé coffee is a Greek iced coffee drink
made from instant coffee (generally, spray-
dried Nescafé), water, sugar, and milk.

Irish

Irish coffee is a cocktail consisting of hot
coffeeIrish, whiskey, and sugar, stirred, and topped
with cream. The coffee is drunk through the cream.

Mocha

A caffè mocha, also called mocaccino, is a
chocolate-flavoured variant of a caffè latte.
Other commonly used spellings are mochaccino
and also mochachino.

Coffeemaker

Coffeemakers or coffee machines are
cooking appliances used to brew coffee. This
is also called automatic drip-brew.

Coffee grinder

The whole coffee beans are ground, also
known as milling, to facilitate the brewing
process. It can also be used as biodiesel fuel.

Coffee Cups

A coffee cup is a container that coffee
and espresso-based drinks are served in. Coffee
cups are typically made of glazed ceramic.

espresso machine

An espresso machine uses high pressure to
force water through coffee within only a few seconds.
Machines may also be manual or automatic.
